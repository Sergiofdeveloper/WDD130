All-inclusive

Youth from invited stakes will only pay €15 for the whole 3 days event. The rest of the cost will be cover by their respective wards.

Payment will be made to their respective bishops through the tithe ballot. The last day to make the payment will be on july 17th.

Every stake will arrange the transportation for their youth and leaders.

Kids won´t need to pay for anything during the event. They are welcome to bring some cash for personal shopping.

Cell Phones and other Electronic Devices

Youth are allowed to bring their mobile to Mini FSY. They will be asked to ONLY use their mobile devices to read the scriptures during the activities.

During free time and outside activities, the use of mobile phones is allowed. However, we recommend that all young people reduce the use of mobile phones to a minimum, even in their free time, in order to encourage socialization with the other participants and avoid the isolation of young people.

We trust that all young people are able to use the mobile phone appropriately and be responsible. If a young person is tempted to use their mobile during an activity for something other than reading the scriptures, we encourage them to use the paper scriptures instead of the mobile.
